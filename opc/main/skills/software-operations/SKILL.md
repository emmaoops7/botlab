# Software Operations

Turn user and market signals into prioritized product or delivery work.

## Inputs

- Feedback, support ticket, usage metric, launch goal, or campaign need
- User segment, channel, frequency, severity, and business impact if known

## Outputs

- Signal brief
- Support triage
- Launch/onboarding plan
- Funnel or retention hypothesis
- Product/engineering recommendation

## Workflow

1. Identify source, affected segment, frequency, and severity.
2. Separate evidence from opinion.
3. Convert complaints into observable problems.
4. Route to product, design, development, tester, release, or DevOps.
5. Define success metric and feedback review point.

## Handoff

Hand off with:
- Audience and problem
- Evidence: quotes, tickets, metrics, screenshots, logs
- Impact and priority
- Recommended next owner
- Success metric or review date

## Done evidence

Done when there is a concise signal brief, priority rationale, next owner, and measurable follow-up.
